Orientation Course

Take your first steps into the digital world and figure out whether you want to start a career in Web Development or Online Marketing.

What is it all about?

Take 4 weeks and find out which digital job suits you best. This course will teach you the basics of Web Development and Online Marketing while preparing you for specialised training and ultimately a job in the digital industry.

What will I learn?

We have designed a detailed curriculum, tailored to all needs. After this course, our students will be able to tell which digital career path is right for them.

Introduction to Web Development Understand how a website works, what a web browser does and learn about various tools, softwares and technologies.

HTML, CSS & JavaScript Learn the fundamentals of HTML, CSS,JS, and find out whether programming is your passion.

Introduction to Online Marketing Understand what Online Marketing is, learn about various marketing channels and what it is like to be a marketing expert.

SEO, SEM, Google Analytics & Facebook Ads Learn basic marketing skills by completing practical exercises using marketing tools and develop knowledge in Email and Content Marketing.
